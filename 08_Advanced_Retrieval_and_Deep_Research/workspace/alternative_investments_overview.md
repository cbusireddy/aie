# Alternative Investments Overview

## Introduction
As investors seek diversification and higher potential returns, alternative investments play a crucial role in portfolio strategies. This document highlights key alternative investment strategies.

## Key Strategies
1. **Real Estate Investments**: Offers income and inflation protection; moderate correlation with equities.
2. **Private Equity**: High returns with longer horizons; diversification from public markets.
3. **Hedge Funds**: Flexible strategies aimed at returns in any market; can hedge risks effectively.
4. **Insurance-Linked Investments**: Uncorrelated with financial markets, providing attractive risk premiums.
5. **Infrastructure Investments**: Stable cash flows; various sectors benefit from government support.

## Conclusion
Allocating 15% of a portfolio to alternative investments can significantly enhance diversification and improve risk-adjusted returns.
