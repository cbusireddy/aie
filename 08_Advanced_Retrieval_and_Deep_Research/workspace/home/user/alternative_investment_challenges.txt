### Key Challenges in Alternative Investments  
  
1. **Regulatory Uncertainties**: The alternative investment landscape is often subject to varying regulations across jurisdictions, complicating investment decisions.  
2. **Performance Volatility**: Certain alternatives can exhibit higher volatility than traditional investments, posing risks to asset valuations.  
3. **Manager Selection**: Identifying skilled managers in private equity and hedge funds is critical but challenging, as many have track records that might be influenced by broader market conditions.
