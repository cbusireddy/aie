### Alternative Investment Strategy

**Objective**: Diversify portfolio by integrating alternative investments for improved risk-adjusted returns and reduced correlation to traditional markets.

#### Current Portfolio Allocation
- **Equities**: 60%
- **Bonds**: 30%
- **Cash**: 10%

#### Proposed Changes
- **Equities**: Decrease to 55%
- **Bonds**: Decrease to 25%
- **Cash**: Decrease to 5%
- **Alternatives**: Introduce at 15%

#### Breakdown of Alternative Investments
1. **Reinsurance (5%)**
   - Objective: Diversification & yield enhancement.
   - Considerations: Non-correlated returns, moderate risk.

2. **Private Equity (6%)**
   - Objective: High returns & access to private markets.
   - Considerations: Long-term lock-up, illiquidity.

3. **Hedge Funds (4%)**
   - Objective: Risk management & absolute returns.
   - Considerations: Manager selection, fees.

4. **Real Assets (3%)**
   - Objective: Income & inflation protection.
   - Considerations: Capital appreciation, medium to long-term horizon.

### Implementation Steps
- **Reallocate funds**: Gradually shift from traditional assets to alternatives.
- **Due Diligence**: Research and select funds/managers with strong track records.
- **Monitor & Review**: Regularly review the performance and adjust allocations as needed.

### Risk Considerations
- Liquidity risk due to long lock-up periods, especially in private equity and real assets.
- Higher fees in hedge funds relative to traditional investments.
- Regulatory and market risks particular to alternative investments.

This strategy aims to enhance the portfolio's long-term performance while balancing risk exposure through diversification into alternative investments.
