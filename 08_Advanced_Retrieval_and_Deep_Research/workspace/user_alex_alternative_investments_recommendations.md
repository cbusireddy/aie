# Alternative Investments Recommendations for Portfolio Diversification

## Objective
To support a diversified investment portfolio with a target of 15% allocation into alternative investments.

## Recommended Allocation
1. **Real Estate Investments**: 5% 
   - Consider Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) for better liquidity.
   - Explore direct ownership opportunities in high-demand markets.

2. **Private Equity**: 5% 
   - Focus on funds with a strong track record in management performance.
   - Include diversified strategies to hedge against market volatility.

3. **Hedge Funds**: 3% 
   - Target funds with low correlation to traditional equity markets.
   - Evaluate varied investment strategies for balance.

4. **Insurance-Linked Investments**: 2% 
   - Invest in catastrophe bonds or collateralized reinsurance.
   - Assess risk capacity based on market conditions.

## Conclusion
Allocating 15% of the portfolio into selected alternative investments strengthens diversification and can potentially improve overall returns amidst fluctuating market conditions.
